# Iron Light Defense
## AI Governance Readiness 
Defense-Informed Oversight Framework for Commercial AI Systems

---

## Overview

Iron Light Defense provides a structured AI governance architecture for organizations deploying advanced AI systems without formal oversight frameworks.

This repository contains:

- Governance doctrine
- AI risk taxonomy model
- Risk scoring methodology
- Governance maturity framework
- Board-level reporting structure
- Deployment-ready governance templates

This framework is conceptually aligned with:

- NIST AI Risk Management principles
- ISO/IEC 42001 governance structures
- Defense-grade risk architecture

AI risk is governance failure.

Establish:

- AI system visibility
- Risk classification
- Control architecture
- Executive oversight
- Incident escalation pathways

## Philosophy

AI must be governed as operational infrastructure.

Not experimental tooling.

Sovereign AI oversight requires:

- Discipline
